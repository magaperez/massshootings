Where are the Exits?

Encountering an active shooter in your place of work or study is unlikely, but it never hurts to prepare for the unthinkable.

In the instance of an active shooter,very second counts.So, remember these three key steps: run, hide and fight

  • Get to safety!

    Move away from the threat

    as quickly as possible.

  • Take the stairs

    If on a higher floor, don’t use elevators -

    windows are also an option

  • If you can’t evacuate

    If you believe you are in the gunman’s line of sight,

    run in a zigzag, or from cover to cover

  • Be quiet

    Find barriers to prevent the shooter from getting to you

  • Above all

    Turn off the lights, silence your phone and call 911

  • Where to hide?

    Anywhere there is a door to lock

The Last Resort

If you can’t hide out and if you have absolutely no other option, confront the active shooter.

Throw or use whatever you have at hand as a weapon against the gunman — scissors, bookends, chairs, a hammer, kettle bells, heavy doorstops. It’s not ideal, but it has helped

Do not fight fairly - Throw items and use improvised weapons
