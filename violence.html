How to Prepare?

Preparation is key to knowing how to react

Self Defense Training

The ability to protect oneself physically from harm is a basic human right that should be accessible to everyone.

It buils confidence & helps you develop a fighters reflex!

Firearm Training for your Safety

Unfortunately, the right to bear arms is enshrined in the Constitution, and there are approximately 265 million privately owned guns in the U.S.

Rules of Gun Safety

- Treat all guns as if they are always loaded

- Never let the muzzle cover anything that you are not willing to destroy.

- Keep your finger off the trigger until your sights are on target and you have made the decision to shoot.

- Be sure of your target and what lies beyond it.

Stay Alert

Be aware of your environment and any possible dangers.

Look for the two nearest exits anywhere you go and & think of places you could hide.
